How it Works

This section explains how our site works.

Creating user

If you are a streamer and want to show some player stats in your scrims or tournaments transmissions, you can here.

Quick Start

Please E-mail us on victor.pubganalysis@gmail.com and we create your username, password and a sheets link. After created, you can log in in our portal, and add the stats to your sheets. After Logged in, you only have to choose the Page in your sheet where the data is and we do the rest.

FAQ

When my data is updated?

We connect to the sheet using Google API, so the data is updated every time. All you need to do is update your sheet and reload the page.
